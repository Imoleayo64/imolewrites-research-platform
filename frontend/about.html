Our story

We build tools that give research back its time

ImoleWrites was founded by researchers who lived the pain — endless literature hunts, broken citations, and tools that never talked to each other. We're fixing that.

How it began

From a frustrating PhD to a global platform

In 2023, our founder spent weeks wrestling with disconnected reference managers, clunky PDF readers, and citation generators that kept getting details wrong. The research itself kept getting squeezed out.

So we built a single, beautiful workspace where AI handles the busywork — searching, citing, refining — so researchers can focus on what matters: ideas, evidence, and discovery.

Today, over 120,000 researchers across 90 countries write and publish with ImoleWrites.

Our Mission

To empower every researcher — from first-year student to seasoned professor — with AI tools that make research smarter, writing better, and publishing faster, while keeping integrity and rigor at the center.

Our Vision

A world where the distance between a spark of curiosity and a published paper is measured in hours, not months — and where great ideas are never lost to broken tools or busywork.
