Adaptive Ensemble Methods for Non-Stationary Climate Projections

Sherlock, B., & Adler, P.

3. Methodology

We propose an adaptive ensemble framework that dynamically re-weights base learners in response to detected distributional shift. Unlike static ensembles, our approach monitors a calibration metric online and triggers re-weighting when drift exceeds a learned threshold.

The framework consists of three components: (i) a diverse pool of base models, (ii) a drift detector based on the Page-Hinkley test, and (iii) a Bayesian re-weighting scheme that preserves uncertainty estimates across regimes.

3.1 Validation Protocol

Central to our contribution is a validation protocol that rewards temporal stability rather than point-in-time accuracy. Models are evaluated on rolling windows drawn from held-out future periods, penalizing performance degradation under regime change.
